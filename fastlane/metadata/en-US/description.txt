Linkagram is a daily word puzzle. Find all the words hidden in a 4x4 grid of letters by swiping to connect adjacent tiles. Each letter can only be used once per word.

A new puzzle is available every day. Track your streak and challenge yourself to find every word. Use hints when you get stuck.

Features:
- New puzzle every day
- Swipe to connect letters and form words
- Track your daily streak
- Use hints to reveal tricky words
- Works fully offline
