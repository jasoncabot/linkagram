Privacy Policy

Linkagram — Last updated: March 7, 2026

Short version: Linkagram does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

Linkagram ("we", "our", or "the app") is a word-puzzle game available at https://linkagram.jasoncabot.me/ and as a native iOS app on the App Store. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

The only data stored is your local game progress, saved using your device's local storage:

  • Words you have found in each puzzle
  • Hints you have used
  • Puzzle start and completion times
  • Your play streak and statistics (games played, completed, best streak)
  • The number of hints remaining in your account

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, not shared with any third party, and not accessible to us.

3. In-App Purchases

Linkagram offers consumable in-app purchases (hint packs) through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information or billing details. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics & Tracking

Linkagram does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or tracking technologies. We do not use cookies or any equivalent tracking mechanisms.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The only external interaction is with Apple's App Store for processing in-app purchases, as described in Section 3.

6. Children's Privacy

Linkagram does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of any age.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by:

  • iOS: Go to Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Linkagram → Delete App, or clear the app's website data in Safari settings.
  • Web browser: Clear your browser's local storage for linkagram.jasoncabot.me via your browser's developer tools or site settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, the updated version will be posted at https://linkagram.jasoncabot.me/privacy.html with a revised "Last updated" date. Because we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.
